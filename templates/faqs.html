How do I apply for engineering colleges in Tamil Nadu?

You can apply through the Tamil Nadu Engineering Admissions (TNEA) counseling process conducted by Anna University. Some colleges also accept JEE Main scores. Register online, fill the application form, and participate in counseling.

Question

What are the documents required for the TNEA counseling process?

The documents include 10th and 12th mark sheets, Transfer Certificate (TC), Community Certificate (if applicable), Income Certificate (for fee concession), Nativity Certificate (if applicable), and JEE Main scorecard (for some colleges).

Answer
Question

Is there any entrance exam required for Tamil Nadu engineering colleges?

For state colleges, TNEA does not have an entrance exam; admissions are based on 12th standard marks. However, some private institutions accept JEE Main scores for admission.
