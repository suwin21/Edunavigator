ABSTRACT

How can Edunavigator help you?

Every year, lakhs of junior college students in Tamil Nadu pass their H.S.C and seek admission to engineering colleges, during the admission process, students must select a minimum number of colleges where they have a chance of gaining entry, consequently, candidates need to compile a list of colleges they wish to apply to, this task often involves extensive manual searching and comparing various college prospects, the goal of this project was to automate the generation of college lists specifically for Tamil Nadu engineering colleges to minimize manual effort by developing a system that can effectively and efficiently generate a list of eligible colleges based on the TNEA TNHSC cut-off and the official database provided by the government, recommendation systems address the problem of Information Overload by helping candidates navigate through large volumes of data.
